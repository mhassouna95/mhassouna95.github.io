About Us

Roos Café & Bistro is a family-owned business that takes pride in serving only the freshest and highest quality food and drinks. Our menu is designed to offer something for everyone, from delicious breakfast options to tasty lunch and dinner items, as well as a selection of sweet treats and beverages.
